Getting Started

You just saw an input form for solving nonlinear equations. To solve any just fill in all empty fields and press the 'Solve' button.

Important caveats

All equations You enter must have only one variable, with 'x' name

In case of any error You'll see the appropriate message (Exception text)

If You are not satisfied with the answer or the answer was not found here is the possible solutions:

  • Change initial x range (left and right bound)
  • Change amount of iterations
  • Change precision
